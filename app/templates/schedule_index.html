Hi!

This is an application to transform your Concordia Schedule into Google calendar events. After you sign-in into you Google account, you will need to grab the url of your schedule, please login on MyConcordia portal. When you get the schedule, copy and paste the url address down here.

If you would like to create a secondary calendar with your schedule, make sure to check the box. The name of the secondary calendar will be "Schedule Concordia".

You will need to accept that this application writes into your Google calendar. This part is done using Oauth. Visit Oauth2 website for more information information about this process.

After this step, you will have the possibility to rollback what has just been done, in case you changed your mind. I am not keeping any information about you or your transaction therefore, make sure to check your calendar and if you are not satisfied with has been done click the rollback link. Orthewise if you want to rollback later you will need to manually delete the events, or the secondary calendar.

Thank you very much for using this application, I hope it was useful, please contact me if you have any problems.
